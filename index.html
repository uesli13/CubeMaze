Cube Maze

How to Play

You are trapped inside a mysterious Cube Maze, a labyrinth that spans all six sides of a giant cube. Your goal is to navigate through the interconnected mazes and find your way to freedom.

Controls:

  • Use W, A, S, D to move forward, left, backward, and right.
  • Move your mouse to look around and explore your surroundings.
